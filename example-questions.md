# Example Test Questions

1. Do autonomous coding agents improve software engineering productivity without increasing defect rates?
2. What does the current academic evidence suggest about retrieval-augmented generation reducing hallucinations in enterprise knowledge tasks?
3. Are multimodal foundation models outperforming specialist systems on medical image triage tasks?
4. Does chain-of-thought prompting consistently improve reasoning benchmarks, or are gains mostly benchmark-specific?
5. Are open-weight LLMs catching up to frontier proprietary models for enterprise workflow automation?
6. What evidence exists that AI copilots improve learning outcomes in higher education?
7. Do LLM-based customer support assistants reduce resolution time without hurting satisfaction?
8. How strong is the evidence that synthetic data improves model robustness in privacy-sensitive domains?
9. Are graph-based retrieval systems outperforming vanilla vector search for complex enterprise Q&A?
10. What does the literature say about AI-generated code security risks in production systems?
